Make your beehive smart!

Monitor and track beehive key metrics by adding “Smart Frame”.

Why



  • 1.Bees are dying rapidly,
  • 2.Traditional beekeeping have no practice of collecting data for further analysis,
  • 3.Insufficient knowledge sharing and experience exchange among the beekeeping community.

Solution



  • 1.Habeetat “Smart Frame“ is designed for Longhorn beehive, equipped with sensors that measure, store and transfers collected data,
  • 2.Data is visually presented through online applications (web and mobile) and provide readings for following metrics:
    • a. Beehive single frame weight (no other solution can do),
    • b. Beehive collective weight (sum of all frames weight values),
    • c. Inside/outside temperature,
    • d. Inside/outside humidity.
  • 3.“Smart Frame“ is true eco product, proven not to be harmful or impactful in any way to bee colonies,
  • 4.Tested in real life conditions in collaboration with professional beekeepers.


Benefits



  • 1.Real-time data monitoring that enables beekeepers to optimize their day-2-day operations,
  • 2.With help of “early-warning” notifications beekeeper is able to:
    • a. Decrease costs of operation,
    • b. Reduce colony loses,
    • c. Detect bee diseases,
    • d. Reduces the honey losses and waste.
  • 3.Transforms beekeeping to “simple to use” app-driven methodology,
  • 4.Collected data is stored and further analyzed enabling big data analysis and prediction modeling,
  • 5.Small switching costs for existing beekeepers with Longhorn beehives,
  • 6.Facilitates beekeepers community interactions and knowledge exchange,

Mission



  • "
    Habeetat core mission is to provide more efficiencies during process of honey production and improvement of bees well-being. Envisioned as the first digital social platform for beekeepers, enabling real-time monitoring, data-driven analysis and personalized notifications.
    "

Habeetat “Smart Frame“

Up to now, producing honey primary relied on the experience and “gut feeling” of the individual beekeepers. Data observed through operation was rarely recorded for future use and analysis. Habeetat aims to change that by creating the first data-driven social platform for the world’s beekeepers and making beekeeping accessible to the wider audience and demographics.

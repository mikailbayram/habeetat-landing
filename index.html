habeetat smart frame


Core of HABBETAT solution is fully integrated hardware and software beehive system for digital beekeeping in 21st century. Its aim is to fully track all vital health and productivity metrics of bee colony. Once installed on your standard Longhorne beehive and activated, habeetat “Smart Frame” measures and stores all key data points such as: temperature, humidity and unlike any other solutions, individual frame weight. With habeetat “Smart Frame” you can convert traditional Longhorne beehives into smart beehive instantly. Habeetat is first technical solution that provides single frame measurement and data collection.

Habeetat mission is to enable more efficient honey production and improve well-being of bees population by creating first centralized online platform for worlds beekeepers interoperable with hardware component (smart i-beehive) through real time monitoring and data analysis. Our core objective is to offer technology solution accessible to wide audience and demographics, especially targeting younger generations initiating their interest in beekeeping activities. We strive to promote new perspective to traditional beekeeping by introducing first mobile application and fully integrated web solution. Hardware component together with online applications, both for web and mobile, are created to be extremely simple to use and user friendly

Make your beehive smart!

Monitor and track key metrics in a real time.

Challenge



  • Bees are dying rapidly
  • Traditional beekeeping has no practice of collecting data for further analysis or future use
  • Estimated 50 million active beehives in the world (90% are Longhorn model)
  • Community with minimal knowledge transfer and experience shearing
  • How to transition from traditional beekeeping into data driven methodologies

Solution



  • Habeetat “Smart Frame“ is adjustable hardware component designed for Longhorn beehives, embedded with sensors that collect, store and transfer data to web and mobile application in real time
  • Uniquely implemented solution that measure all key data points:
    • Beehive single frame weight (no other solution can do)
    • Beehive collective weight (sum of all frames weight values)
    • Inside/outside temperature
    • Inside/outside humidity
  • “Smart Frame“ is designed not to be harmful or impactful in any way to bee colonies (true eco product)
  • Solution has been tested and proven in real life conditions in collaboration with professional beekeepers


Benefits



  • Real time data monitoring enables beekeepers to optimize their day-2-day operations, making the entire process far more efficient
  • With “early-warning” notifications, beekeepers can:
    • Decrease costs of operation
    • Reduce colony loses
    • Detect bee diseases
    • Improve well-being of bee population
    • Reduces the honey losses and waste
  • Promoting beekeeping as more accessible to the general population by introducing “simple to use” app driven methodologies
  • Key data is analyzed and visually presented
  • Small switching costs, solution works as add on to existing set up
  • Prediction modeling based on big data analysis on platform level
  • Beekeepers social community interactions and knowledge exchange

Mission



  • Regardless if you proven beekeeper or just starting, habeetat is an open online community that helps you to connect with other beekeepers, interact and exchange experiences and best practices

habeetat “Smart Frame“

Up to now, primary way of producing honey relied heavily on the experience and “gut feeling” of the individual beekeepers, utilizing traditional agricultural methods of placing the hives at a locations that are believed to be most suitable for higher honey gain. Additionally, data collected through its utilization is rarely recorded for future use and analysis.

HaBEEtat aims to change that. It’s core mission is to enable more efficient honey production and improve the well-being of bees, by creating first integrated online platform for beekeepers, enabling real time monitoring and data driven analysis and prediction modeling. With a such approach, beekeeping will become more accessible to wider audience and demographics, in particular younger generations, reigniting their interest for beekeeping. Habeetat mission is to create the first data driven social platform for world’s beekeepers.
